Admission / Registration Criteria

All prospective students seeking admission must be holders of a GCE «A» level or a Bac certificates or their equivalents.

NB: All arts students and holders of Bac G1, G3 & A can only be admitted for NURSING or MIDWIFERY programs only for medical and biomedical science students.
Year 2 & 3 (level 200 & 300): All transferred cases into these levels are considered on an individual basis.
